HOW TO PLAY

Basics

Place an ante bet, then 3 cards are dealt to you (face-up) and the dealer (face-down).

Play or Fold

  • PLAY — costs 1× your ante. Dealer reveals cards.
  • FOLD — forfeit your ante bet.

Dealer Qualification

Dealer needs Queen-high or better to qualify.

  • Doesn't qualify: ante pays 1:1, play bet returned.
  • Qualifies & you win: both ante and play pay 1:1.
  • Qualifies & dealer wins: lose both bets.
  • Tie: all bets returned (push).

Hand Rankings (Highest → Lowest)

  • Prial — Three of a kind (3-3-3 is HIGHEST!)
  • Running Flush — Straight flush (A-2-3 beats A-K-Q)
  • Run — Straight (A-2-3 beats A-K-Q)
  • Flush — Three cards of same suit
  • Pair — Two matching cards + kicker
  • High Card — Highest card wins
