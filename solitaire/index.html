HOW TO PLAY

Objective

Move all 52 cards to the four foundation piles, sorted by suit from Ace to King.

The Layout

  • DRAW — Click to flip one card at a time from the stock.
  • WASTE — The top card is always available to play.
  • Foundations (♠♥♦♣) — Build each suit up from Ace to King.
  • Tableau — Seven columns. Build down in alternating colours.

Moving Cards

  • Click a card to select it, then click a valid destination.
  • Drag cards directly to their destination.
  • Double-click any face-up card to send it to a foundation automatically.
  • Only Kings may be placed on empty tableau columns.
  • Face-down cards flip automatically when the card above is moved.

Auto-Complete

When stock is empty and all tableau cards are face-up, the game finishes itself automatically.

Scoring

  • Base: 500 points · Time bonus: up to +300 · Speed bonus: +100 (under 3 min)
  • Move penalty: −2 per move
