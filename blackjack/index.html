🃏 HOW TO PLAY BLACKJACK

Objective

Beat the dealer by getting a hand value closer to 21 without going over (busting).

Card Values

  • Number cards (2–10) = face value
  • Jack, Queen, King = 10
  • Ace = 1 or 11 (whichever helps most)

How a Hand Works

  • Place your bet, then press DEAL
  • You get 2 cards face up. Dealer gets 1 face up, 1 face down (hole card)
  • HIT — draw another card
  • STAND — keep your hand, dealer reveals and plays
  • DOUBLE — double your bet, draw exactly one more card, then stand
  • SPLIT — if your first two cards are equal value, split into two separate hands

Dealer Rules

Dealer must hit on 16 or less, and stand on 17 or more.

Payouts

  • Win = 1:1 (bet returned + equal winnings)
  • Blackjack (Ace + 10-value on first 2 cards) = 3:2
  • Push (tie) = bet returned
  • Bust or dealer wins = bet lost

Blackjack

An Ace + any 10-value card on your initial 2 cards = Blackjack. Pays 3:2 unless dealer also has Blackjack (push).
