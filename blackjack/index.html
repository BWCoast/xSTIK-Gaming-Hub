HOW TO PLAY

Objective

Beat the dealer by getting a hand value as close to 21 as possible without going over.

Card Values

  • Number cards (2–10) = face value
  • Face cards (J, Q, K) = 10
  • Ace = 1 or 11 (automatically optimized)

Gameplay

  • Place a bet using chips (10, 25, 50, 100)
  • You and the dealer each receive 2 cards
  • One dealer card is face-down (the hole card)
  • HIT — draw another card
  • STAND — keep your current hand
  • DOUBLE DOWN — double your bet, receive exactly one more card, then stand

Winning

  • Blackjack (Ace + 10-value on first two cards) pays 3:2
  • Beat the dealer's hand = win 1:1
  • Dealer busts (over 21) = you win
  • Push (tie) = bet returned
  • Dealer stands on 17 or higher

Credits

Bet increments: 10, 25, 50, 100. Stack chips to increase your bet. Winnings are added to your credit balance.
