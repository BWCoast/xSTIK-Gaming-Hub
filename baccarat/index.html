HOW TO PLAY BACCARAT

Objective

Bet on which hand — Player or Banker — will have a total closest to 9, or bet on a Tie.

Card Values

  • Ace = 1
  • 2–9 = Face value
  • 10, J, Q, K = 0

Hand value = sum of cards mod 10 (only the last digit counts).

Natural

If either hand totals 8 or 9 on the first two cards, it's a "natural" — no more cards are drawn.

Drawing Rules

Player: Stands on 6–7, draws on 0–5.

Banker: Complex rules based on own total and Player's third card.

Payouts

  • Player wins: 1:1
  • Banker wins: 0.95:1 (5% commission)
  • Tie: 8:1

Betting

Select a chip value, then click Player, Banker, or Tie to place your bet. Click DEAL when ready.
