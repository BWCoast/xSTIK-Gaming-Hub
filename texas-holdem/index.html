HOW TO PLAY

Objective

Make the best 5-card poker hand using any combination of your 2 hole cards and the 5 community cards. Win the pot!

The Blinds

Each hand, two players post forced bets: the Small Blind (SB) and the Big Blind (BB). The dealer button rotates each hand.

Betting Rounds

  • Pre-Flop — After hole cards are dealt. Action starts left of the Big Blind.
  • Flop — 3 community cards revealed. Bet, check, call, raise, or fold.
  • Turn — 4th community card. Another betting round.
  • River — 5th and final community card. Last betting round.
  • Showdown — Remaining players reveal hands. Best hand wins the pot.

Your Actions

  • Check — Pass the action (only if no bet to call).
  • Call — Match the current bet.
  • Raise — Increase the bet. Use the slider to set your amount.
  • All In — Bet all your remaining chips.
  • Fold — Surrender your hand and forfeit the pot.
