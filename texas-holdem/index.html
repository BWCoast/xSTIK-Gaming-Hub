HOW TO PLAY

Overview

Texas Hold'Em heads-up against an AI opponent. Make the best 5-card hand from your 2 hole cards and 5 community cards.

Blinds

Small blind: 10 credits (AI). Big blind: 20 credits (you). The big blind is your entry fee each hand.

Rounds

  • Pre-Flop: 2 hole cards dealt. First betting round.
  • Flop: 3 community cards revealed. Second betting.
  • Turn: 4th community card. Third betting.
  • River: 5th community card. Final betting.
  • Showdown: Best 5-card hand wins the pot.

Actions

  • Check: Pass (if no bet to call).
  • Bet: Place a wager.
  • Call: Match the current bet.
  • Raise: Increase the current bet.
  • Fold: Surrender the hand.

Hand Rankings

Royal Flush → Straight Flush → Four of a Kind → Full House → Flush → Straight → Three of a Kind → Two Pair → One Pair → High Card
