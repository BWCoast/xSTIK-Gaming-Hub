HOW TO PLAY

Objective

Clear all 28 cards from the pyramid by removing pairs that add up to 13.

Card Values

  • Ace = 1
  • 2–10 = face value
  • Jack = 11, Queen = 12
  • King = 13 (removed alone)

How to Play

  • Click an exposed card to select it
  • Click a second exposed card — if they sum to 13, both are removed
  • Click a King to remove it instantly
  • Click the stock pile to draw a card to the waste pile
  • The top waste card can be paired with exposed pyramid cards
  • A card is exposed when both cards below it are removed

Winning

Clear the entire pyramid to win 200 credits. Entry fee: 50 credits.
