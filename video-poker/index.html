HOW TO PLAY

Jacks or Better

Classic 5-card draw video poker. Get a pair of Jacks or better to win!

Gameplay

  • Choose your bet: 10, 20, or 50 credits
  • Click DEAL to receive 5 cards
  • Click cards to HOLD the ones you want to keep
  • Click DRAW — non-held cards are replaced
  • Your final hand is evaluated and winnings paid
